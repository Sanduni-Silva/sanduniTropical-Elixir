Our Products

Coconut Oil

Meticulously extracted from the finest coconuts, our coconut oil embodies purity and richness. Whether used for culinary masterpieces or as a beauty elixir, Tropical Elixar's coconut oil is a testament to our commitment to quality.

Coconut Water

Our coconut water captures the essence of tropical paradise in every sip, offering a hydrating and rejuvenating experience. Perfectly balancing sweetness and freshness, it's a natural source of vitality.

Coconut Milk Powder

Crafted with precision, our coconut milk powder retains the authentic taste of coconut milk, ready to elevate culinary creations with a tropical twist. From curries to desserts, it's a versatile ingredient that adds a touch of the exotic to any dish.

Coconut Butter

Our velvety coconut butter encapsulates the essence of coconut in a rich, creamy texture. Ideal for both sweet and savory applications, it's a luscious addition to breakfast spreads or gourmet recipes.
